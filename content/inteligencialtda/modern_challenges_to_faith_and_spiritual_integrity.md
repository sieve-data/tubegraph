*   **Ordinary Activity:** Temptation to sin through suggestion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:59:00">[00:59:00]</a>. This is a common spiritual battle <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:00:00">[01:00:00]</a>.

## Safeguarding Spiritual Integrity

2.  **Daily Mass and Communion:** Considered "spiritual Red Bull," Mass provides powerful graces and communion fills the soul with God <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:51:00">[00:51:00]</a>. To receive communion well, one must be confessed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:53:00">[00:53:00]</a>.
