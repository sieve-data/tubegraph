## Moving Beyond Text Prompting
Initially, [[ai_tools_like_midjourney_and_chat_gpt | Midjourney]] primarily relied on text prompts and [[parameters_and_controls_for_achieving_desired_artistic_outcomes_with_midjourney | parameters]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:55:07">[01:55:07]</a>. While words are still valuable, they represent "just the tip of the iceberg" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:07:07">[01:07:07]</a>. The true potential lies in using visuals to drive visuals, allowing users to "explode out aesthetics and style space into almost infinite directions" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:12:00">[01:12:00]</a>.

Recent developments in [[ai_tools_like_midjourney_and_chat_gpt | Midjourney]] have introduced:
*   **Style Space**: A concept that expands aesthetics into an infinite universe <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:29:00">[02:29:00]</a>.
*   **[[using_style_references_and_personalization_in_midjourney | Style References]]**: The ability to use images as aesthetic guides, influencing the generated output's style <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:22:00">[02:22:00]</a>. This differs from simple image prompts, which pull in subject matter and elements, potentially making results "wonky" if the intent is only style <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:03:00">[02:03:00]</a>. Style references decouple aesthetics from subject matter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:52:00">[01:52:00]</a>.
*   **Character References**: Used for maintaining consistent character appearance (not covered in detail) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:24:00">[02:24:00]</a>.
*   **SRF Codes**: These are "coordinates in this multi-dimensional Style Space" that direct prompts to a specific aesthetic location <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:37:00">[03:37:00]</a>. There are billions of possible SRF codes that people are actively mining and curating into "aesthetic recipes" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:08:00">[03:08:00]</a>.

## Achieving Desired Artistic Outcomes
The core principle in [[creating_art_with_ai_and_blending_aesthetics_in_midjourney | creating art with AI]] in [[ai_tools_like_midjourney_and_chat_gpt | Midjourney]] is finding "balance and harmony" among various aesthetic levers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:29:00">[01:29:00]</a>. These include:

### Parameters
[[parameters_and_controls_for_achieving_desired_artistic_outcomes_with_midjourney | Parameters]] are used to guide and push aesthetics in specific directions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:31:00">[01:31:00]</a>.
*   **Style Weight (`--sw`)**: Determines the influence of a [[using_style_references_and_personalization_in_midjourney | style reference]] on the generation, ranging from 100 (default) to 1000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:17:00">[01:17:00]</a>.
*   **Aspect Ratio (`--ar`)**: Controls the image's dimensions, useful for specific outputs like posters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:57:00">[01:57:00]</a>.
*   **No Parameter (`--no`)**: Tells [[ai_tools_like_midjourney_and_chat_gpt | Midjourney]] what elements to remove from the generation, such as unwanted text <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:52:00">[02:52:00]</a>.
*   **Stylize (`--s`)**: Controls how much of the [[ai_tools_like_midjourney_and_chat_gpt | Midjourney]] model's curated aesthetics are applied to the text prompt. Higher values make the image look "more like Midjourney" or "scream AI" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:20:00">[03:20:00]</a>.
*   **Permutation Prompts**: Using brackets and commas `{option1, option2}` allows running multiple [[parameters_and_controls_for_achieving_desired_artistic_outcomes_with_midjourney | parameter]] values in one go for quick visual comparison <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:49:00">[04:49:00]</a>.

### [[using_style_references_and_personalization_in_midjourney | Personalization]]
[[using_style_references_and_personalization_in_midjourney | Personalization]] fine-tunes the [[ai_tools_like_midjourney_and_chat_gpt | Midjourney]] model to a user's aesthetic preferences <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:31:00">[03:31:00]</a>. This is achieved by rating images in the "Personalize" tab, teaching [[ai_tools_like_midjourney_and_chat_gpt | Midjourney]] about preferred colors, lighting, general style (photographic vs. illustrative), and even specific subjects <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:39:00">[03:39:00]</a>. Rating a thousand images can significantly improve aesthetic alignment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:41:00">[03:41:00]</a>.

### Blending Styles
[[ai_tools_like_midjourney_and_chat_gpt | Midjourney]] allows for [[creating_art_with_ai_and_blending_aesthetics_in_midjourney | blending aesthetics]] by combining multiple [[using_style_references_and_personalization_in_midjourney | style references]] or SRF codes in a single prompt <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:82:00">[02:82:00]</a>. Users can assign specific weights to each style using double colons (e.g., `srf_code::2 srf_code_2::1`) to control their influence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:45:00">[04:45:00]</a>. SRF codes represent complex aesthetic qualities like color, composition, lighting, texture, and pattern <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:19:00">[04:19:00]</a>.
